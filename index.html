Bring Me the Horizon is a British rock band known for
their versatile and powerful sound. They started in 2004
as a deathcore band but quickly evolved by incorporating
influences from metal, pop, and electronic music. Led by
vocalist Oli Sykes, the band has grown into a global
success with a reputation for energetic live performances
and several hit albums
